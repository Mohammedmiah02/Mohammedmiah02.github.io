Glacier Experience is a small group experience that offers an easy glacier hike on Solheimajokull glacier with an experienced glacier guide and is available all year round. The tour offers a pick up from Reykjavik or a meet on location at Solheimajokull glacier. Add even more adventure to your stay in Iceland and come explore this amazing crawling glacier with us

Solheimajokull glacier is an outlet from the mighty Myrdalsojkull and is surrounded by some of Iceland's most volcanoes and during summertime, a glacier lagoon forms in front of the glacier only adding to the charm of the location. This popular day tour is perfect way to get in real contact with the true reason behind the name Ice-land
